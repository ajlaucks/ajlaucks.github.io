Misc. Thoughts

Time: 2021–2023

straight lines: There are no straight lines in nature, but why? Why do straight lines appear in human constructions? Maybe it is because of the inexhaustible desire of the human spirit to understand, to abstract, and to know. The line contains a minute amount of information when compared to any natural form. Start point, endpoint. That is it. In the desire to understand and operate within the world, we construct our own worlds which we can process completely.

procedural design: Procedural design (based on a process that creates form as opposed to an agent consciously creating form) is but a reversal of the straight line. While the forms it creates are seemingly more complex, the conscious agency of the designer is directed towards a process that is just as simple, or even more simple, than the two points of the line.

subset: We live in a subset of the real world / A lie evolution has constructed for us out of pure necessity.

meaning: Treat life as if the very fabric of the cosmos yearns for your contribution / Treat every person as if they matter / Treat everything you do as if it matters / Treat each moment as if it matters / Because the funny thing is / All you have to do is believe / And it will.

truth: The lie all believe is the truth.
