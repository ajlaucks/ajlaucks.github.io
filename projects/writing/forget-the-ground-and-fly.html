Forget the Ground and Fly

Time: March 2021

A herd we were / For timeless eons / Together against the strife / of nature / Yet something changed / Untold years ago / To make us unique / Unlike the past

I is what makes us unique / Yet us is what got us here / Should we abandon the herd so quickly / To walk alone / In uncharted lands / Or pay homage to our roots

Evolution must again / Play its part / To separate us / From those before / We are but a bridge / To the omnipotence / That the future / Brings / A rope stretched out / over a deep chasm / Full of the darkness / That yearns to take us / With time we will forget the ground and fly
